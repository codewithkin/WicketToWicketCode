How do I choose the right cricket gloves ?

Cricket gloves are crucial for hand protection while batting and keeping a good grip on the bat. Look for gloves that offer ample padding on the fingers, palm, and thumb to absorb impact from the ball. Consider the fit, ensuring they are snug but not too tight, and provide enough flexibility for gripping the bat comfortably. Also, check the durability of the materials and stitching to ensure they last through many matches and practice sessions.
